Wireless Melody Player

A system comprising of a mobile phone application, two Raspberry Pis and some circuitry. The idea being that the mobile application acts as a musical keyboard input. These inputs are then transmitted to one Pi, who then uses infrared communication to transmit to the next Pi, who outputs the signal through an amplifier and speaker circuit.
